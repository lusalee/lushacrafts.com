Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering artificial plants from Lusha Crafts

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ is flexible and varies by product:

  • Artificial Olive Trees & Ficus: 20 pieces per design (can mix heights)
  • Silk Orchids: 100 stems per color (can mix colors)
  • Artificial Palm Trees: 5 pieces per design (custom projects)
  • Custom Designs: Negotiable based on complexity

We accommodate both trial orders and bulk shipments. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Are your artificial plants UV-resistant?

Yes. Our outdoor artificial plants use advanced UV-resistant PE materials that are tested under direct sunlight. We offer a 3-year guarantee against fading and cracking for all outdoor-rated products.

SGS test reports for UV resistance are available on request. Indoor products do not require UV treatment, which makes them more cost-effective.

Can I get samples before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely — and we strongly recommend it. Photos can't fully capture texture, color accuracy, and quality. Here's how it works:

  • Sample cost: Charged at regular unit price + shipping
  • Sample cost is fully refundable against your bulk order
  • Production samples (exact match to your order spec) available after order confirmation
Do you offer custom designs?

Yes — custom design is one of our core strengths. We can create:

  • Custom sizes & shapes — any height, any form factor
  • Custom colors — match your brand palette or decor theme
  • Custom packaging — retail-ready with your branding, UPC codes, hang tags
  • Custom species — send us a photo or sketch, we'll build it
What are your payment terms?

Our standard payment terms:

  • 30% deposit to confirm order and start production
  • 70% balance before shipment (after QC approval)
  • We accept T/T bank transfer and L/C (Letter of Credit)

What shipping terms do you offer?

We offer three shipping terms to fit your needs:

  • FOB Shenzhen — Best if you have your own freight forwarder
  • CIF — We arrange shipping and insurance to your destination port
  • DDP — Door-to-door delivery (available for select countries)

We ship regularly to the Middle East, Europe, Africa, South Asia, and the Americas.

Can I visit your factory?

Yes — we welcome factory visits! Seeing is believing in the artificial plants business. You can:

  • Tour our 3,000+ sqm production facility
  • Inspect products in person — touch, feel, compare
  • Meet our design and QC teams
  • Discuss custom requirements face-to-face

Dongguan is 1 hour from Shenzhen Bao'an Airport and 1.5 hours from Hong Kong. Contact us to schedule — we'll arrange pickup.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes. We provide full OEM and ODM services:

  • OEM: Produce our designs with your branding and packaging
  • ODM: Design and produce entirely new products to your specifications
  • Custom packaging: branded boxes, UPC codes, hang tags, display boxes
  • Confidentiality: Your designs remain yours — we don't share with other clients
What quality certifications do you have?

Quality is our top priority. We provide:

  • SGS test reports — UV resistance, material safety, durability
  • In-house QC — Multi-stage inspection at every production phase
  • Third-party inspection — Bureau Veritas, SGS, or your preferred agency
  • Pre-shipment photos & videos — We document every order before loading
