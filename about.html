About Lusha Crafts

Your trusted artificial plants manufacturing partner in Dongguan, China

Who We Are

Dongguan Lusha Crafts Co., Ltd. is a professional artificial plants manufacturer based in Dongguan, Guangdong — the heart of China's manufacturing industry. Since 2015, we have been designing, producing, and exporting high-quality artificial trees, silk flowers, and custom artificial plants to B2B buyers worldwide.

We are a real factory, not a trading company. When you work with Lusha Crafts, you deal directly with the manufacturer — no middlemen, no markup, and full control over quality and production. Our 3,000+ sqm production facility in Dongguan is equipped with advanced molding, assembly, and QC equipment.
