How to Import Artificial Plants from China — Step by Step

Importing artificial plants from China can seem complex, but it's straightforward when you work with an experienced manufacturer. Here's our recommended process:

Step 1: Define Your Requirements

Before contacting a factory, clarify:

  • Product types — Olive trees? Ficus? Orchids? Custom designs?
  • Quantities — Trial order (20–50 pcs) or bulk container (500+ pcs)?
  • Target price range — Helps us recommend the right product grade
  • Usage — Indoor or outdoor? UV-resistant needed?
  • Timeline — When do you need delivery?

Step 2: Request Quote & Samples

Send us your requirements. We'll provide:

  • Product photos and detailed specifications
  • FOB/CIF price quote based on your quantity
  • Production lead time estimate
  • Sample options — we can ship physical samples for your evaluation

Tip: Always request physical samples before placing a bulk order. Photos can't fully capture texture and quality.

Step 3: Confirm Order & Payment

Once you're satisfied with samples:

  • Proforma Invoice — We issue a PI with full order details
  • Payment Terms — Typically 30% deposit to start production, 70% balance before shipment (T/T or L/C)
  • Production — 15–25 days for standard products, 25–35 days for custom designs

Step 4: Quality Inspection

Quality assurance before shipping:

  • In-house QC checks at every production stage
  • Pre-shipment inspection with photos and videos sent to you
  • Third-party inspection (SGS, Bureau Veritas) available at your request
  • You're welcome to visit our factory for in-person inspection

Step 5: Shipping & Delivery

We offer flexible shipping terms:

  • FOB Shenzhen — You arrange shipping; we handle everything up to the port
  • CIF — We arrange shipping to your destination port
  • DDP — Door-to-door delivery available for select countries
  • Standard sea freight: 20–35 days depending on destination
  • Air freight available for urgent/small orders
